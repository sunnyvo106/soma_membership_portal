## 1. Purpose
This document defines the functional and non-functional solution requirements for the Soma Membership Portal. It translates approved business requirements into clear specifications to guide design, development, testing, and implementation.

---

## 2. Solution Overview
The Soma Membership Portal is a secure, web and mobile-accessible platform that enables:
- Online membership registration and verification
- Secure authentication and profile management
- E-wallet creation and payment integration
- Loan request submission
- Community project management
- Administrative reporting and approvals

## 5. Non-Functional Requirements

### Security
- Data encrypted in transit and at rest
- Role-based access control
- ISO-aligned security standards

### Performance
- Support 100+ concurrent users
- Page load < 3 seconds under normal load

### Availability
- 24/7 system availability

### Compatibility
- Works across modern browsers and devices

---

## 6. Assumptions & Dependencies
- Regulatory approval obtained before go-live
- Third-party payment services available
- Users have internet access
